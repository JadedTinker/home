Track meals, symptoms & macros to uncover your food triggers.

FoodTrace is a food and symptom diary in one app. Log what you eat, how you feel, and the small daily things that affect your body — then look back later to see patterns you would otherwise miss.

Works offline No account required Free cloud sync Android

What you get

Food & meal logging

Add ingredients by meal with autocomplete from your history. Pinned foods, meal templates, and a barcode scanner for packaged products.

Nutrition that's honest

8,000+ foods from the USDA FoodData Central database. Calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, and water. Per-100g or estimated-portion display.

Digestion & symptoms

Log heartburn, bloating, nausea, cramps, and more on a 1–10 scale. Bristol Stool Scale, mood, energy, stress, sleep, and hydration.

Trends & patterns

7, 30, and 90-day macro charts with an optional symptom overlay. A symptom heatmap, side-by-side meal comparison, and streak tracking.

Elimination protocol

Guided elimination, reintroduction, and stabilization phases. Works for low-FODMAP, gluten, dairy, or your own protocol.

Backup & sync

Fully offline first. Optional free cloud sync after sign-in. Local JSON backup with checksum and CSV export.

Premium adds ingredient–symptom correlations, photo meal logging, unlimited meal templates, clinical PDF export, and AI focus summaries.

Not a medical device

FoodTrace is for personal tracking and reflection. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If a symptom worries you, talk to a qualified healthcare professional.
